Center for Computational Media Intelligence

Signal Analysis and Interpretation Lab

The Center for Computational Media Intelligence (CCMI) group is involved in developing novel computational methods to uncover elements of content impact and experience in multimodal multimedia sources from a human-centric perspective.

In this website you will find comprehensive information on current and past research projects, current and past members of our team, as well as useful resources and publications we share with the community.

Our collaborators include Geena Davis Institute on Gender in Media, Google, University of Southern California and United States Chamber of Commerce (USCC) Foundation,
